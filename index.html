    About

    Michelle E. Samson, CEO and Founder of Vagari Consulting, is a creative and analytical thinker with over a decade of experience helping a variety of clients, big and small, elevated and down to earth. A while ago, she closed the door on a well-paying and demanding digital marketing career in NYC because her heart just wasn’t in it. She loved the work— because there’s nothing more exciting than generating $1M in sales with one email or creating a complementary web experience for a beauty pop-up show in one of the world’s largest department stores—but she wanted to start her own business again, to have the freedom to choose her own clients, and make a difference.
    Today, Michelle is putting her innovative process, attention to detail, and ability to thoughtfully diagnose and treat organizational gaps to work for her clients. Her breadth of experience as an Editor and Photography Editor, Financial Reporter, PR Account Executive, Small Business Owner, Copywriter, and Senior Manager have uniquely positioned her to be a tremendously well-rounded resource.
    In Michelle’s spare time she enjoys running, hiking, traveling, reading, partaking in some amateur rescue-dog photography, and playing rec league soccer that she probably takes a little too seriously (left over competitiveness from her college days). She also has an *almost* unhealthy love for alpacas, hence the inspiration behind our mascot.
